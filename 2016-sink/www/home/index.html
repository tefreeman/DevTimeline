... Bring the Sink experience to your home or business ...

To get started as a user, simply register for Sink, update your ToDo with a description and photo of your project, and vetted, experienced Sink professionals nearby will contact you with a quote for the job.

We connect you with local craftspeople with the necessary expertise to do your project right, the first time, every time, at a lower cost and higher accountability than traditional repair and maintenance companies.

... Our Mission ...

At Sink, we are true believers in the American Dream. We are devoted and inspired by the ideals of self-sufficiency, entrepreneurship, and camaraderie. Our platform enables and empowers people to harness their skills and to make money for themselves on their own terms. By extending the ability to offer wide-ranging services and putting it inside a web-based platform, we are connecting people with problems to neighbors with solutions. Our state-of-the-art location system allows you to find local professionals with the skills to tackle your toughest projects, while supporting small business and self-employed craftspeople.

We hold very strongly to the idea of community here at Sink. Our primary objective is to connect people to each other in a way that hasn’t been seen for many years. We might hear stories from grandparents how they could always talk to their neighbor when their faulty bathroom light blew out or their car needed fixing, but in this digital age that phenomenon is quite rare. We’re trying to revitalize and revive that ideal by giving users the ability to find local experts that can help them with their projects, support their neighbors, foster growth of practical skills and trades in their communities, and bring people closer together.
